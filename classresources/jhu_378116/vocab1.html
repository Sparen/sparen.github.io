単語(たんご) (Lessons 1-12) - Sentences (J->E)

Answers are hidden by spoilers. Click the buttons below the text fields to show a suggested answer.

1) Provide a translation for the following: 来学期(らいがっき)、日本語(にほんご)をとるつもりですか？

Next semester, do you plan to take Japanese?

2) Provide a translation for the following: たばこを吸(す)わないでください

Please do not smoke tobacco.

3) Provide a translation for the following: 喉(のど)が渇(かわ)くときに水を飲んだ方がいいですよ。

When you are thirsty, it's a good idea to drink water.

4) Provide a translation for the following: 元気(げんき)じゃないですね。お大事(だいじ)に。

You don't look well. Get well soon.

5) Provide a translation for the following: 心配(しんぱい)しないで。BME in the Real Worldはとても簡単(かんたん)ですよ！

Don't worry! BME in the Real World is very easy!
