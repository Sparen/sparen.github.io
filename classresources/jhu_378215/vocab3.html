単語(たんご) (Lessons 13-15) - Sentences (J->E)

Answers are hidden by spoilers. Click the buttons below the text fields to show a suggested answer.

1) Provide a translation for the following: 韓国(かんこく)へ行った経験(けいけん)がありますか?

Have you had the experience of going to Korea?

2) Provide a translation for the following: 日本語の先生は厳(きび)しそうですね。勉強(べんきょう)しなければいけません。

[Our] Japanese teacher seems strict, right? [We] must study.

3) Provide a translation for the following:

漫画(まんが)が六冊(ろくさつ)あります。同(おな)じじゃないけど、母(はは)は「同じだから、どうして買っていますか」と言っていました。

I have 6 volumes of manga. They aren't the same, but my mother said "Because they're all the same, why are you buying them?"

4) Provide a translation for the following: あきらめないで！やくざになりたいので、頑張(がんば)ってね！

Don't give up! Because you want to become a Yakuza, try your best!

5) Provide a translation for the following: その旅館(りょかん)は庭(にわ)がとても広(ひろ)いと聞きました。

I've heard that that inn has a very wide garden.

6) Provide a translation for the following:

保険(ほけん)は高すぎるし、弁護士(べんごし)に会いたくないし、地震(じしん)がたくさんあるし、ここに住みたくありません。

Because insurance is expensive, I don't want to meet a lawyer, and because there are a lot of earthquakes, I don't want to live here.

7) Provide a translation for the following: かわいい猫(ねこ)が欲(ほ)しいです。でも、両親(りょうしん)はとてもけちです。

I want a cute cat. However, [my] parents are really stingy.

8) Provide a translation for the following: お土産(みやげ)を友達(ともだち)にあげてください

Please give the souvenirs (gifts) to your friends.
