単語(たんご) (Lessons 19-21) - Sentences (J->E)

Answers are hidden by spoilers. Click the buttons below the text fields to show a suggested answer.

1) Provide a translation for the following: 病院(びょういん)で医者(いしゃ)と話(はなし)をする間(あいだ)に泥棒(どろぼう)にテレビを盗(ぬす)まれました。

While taking to the doctor at the hospital, the television was stolen by a thief.

2) Provide a translation for the following: アカメが斬(き)るというアニメは誰(だれ)が犯人(はんにん)ですか？

In the anime called Akame ga Kill, who's the criminal?

3) Provide a translation for the following: 犬(いぬ)に気(き)が付(つ)かれました。

I was noticed by the dog.

4) Provide a translation for the following: 終電(しゅうでん)に乗(の)り遅(おく)れたので同僚(どうりょう)の電子辞書(でんしじしょ)を戻(もど)れませんでした。

Since I missed the last train, I was unable to return my colleague's electronic dictionary.

5) Provide a translation for the following: 趣味(しゅみ)は小説(しょうせつ)を書(か)くけど怠(なま)け者(もの)だから全然(ぜんぜん)書きません。

Writing novels is my hobby but since I am a lazy person, I never write anything.

6) Provide a translation for the following: 蚊(か)に刺(さ)される時(とき)に、いつも文句(もんく)を言(い)います。

When I am bitten by a mosquito, I complain.

7) Provide a translation for the following: 同僚(どうりょう)の杉山(すぎやま)さんと部長(ぶちょう)は仲(なか)がいいのでちょっとうらやましいです。

My colleague Sugiyama and the department manager get along well, so I'm a little jealous.

8) Provide a translation for the following: 少々(しょうしょう)係の物(かかりのもの)が参(まい)ります。

In a few seconds, the person in charge will arrive (humble).

9) Provide a translation for the following: 信号(しんごう)で左(ひだり)に曲(ま)がってくださいませんか。

Won't you please turn left at the traffic light?

10) Provide a translation for the following: 壊(こわ)された扇子(せんす)を返品(へんぴん)してください。

Please return the fan that was broken (adversely).
