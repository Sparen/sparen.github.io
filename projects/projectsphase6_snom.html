Snomnomnom
The Trial of Snomsnom



RELEASE INFORMATION

Release Date: May 08, 2020

Download: Download 1.0 (May 08, 2020)

Install: Place the unarchived directory within the 'script' directory of your copy of Touhou Danmakufu ph3 [.1]

GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

Snomnomnom ~ The Trial of Snomsnom is a two stage adventure featuring Snomsnom, a Snom, as the player. The first part is a (much delayed) April Fools 2020 script with (perhaps surprisingly) serious danmaku, while the second part is a serious, full playing field Hard/Extra difficulty script requiring mastery of the player gimmick.

The player gimmick comes in the form of 'Snomlocking', a process by which the familiars destroy bullets of a similar color. This is based on the delay color specified in the shotsheet. The player has an outer ring of blue-removing familiars and an inner ring of green-removing familiars. The bomb destroys all bullets that can be destroyed by a bomb, and also turns red bullets against the enemy.

There are no difficulty settings for this script. Items are gained after each attack - a life shard if the player did not die (bombs are OK) and a spell shard otherwise. Full extends and bombs are dropped during the scripts.
