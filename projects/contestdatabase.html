Contest Database

Welcome to the unofficial database for Danmakufu Contests!

Over the years, various contests have been held, both public and private. And in each case, we continuously spew out more and more danmaku in increasingly copious quantities. I am making this 'database' as a way for people to actually *find* contests.

The database was originally compiled and prepared in late January-early February 2016.

Contests are only added into the JSON database once they have finished and/or the submission deadline has passed. Planned or In-Progress contests will be in RED rather than AQUA, which is used for Completed contests.

Contests are sorted by hosting location - the location where they were hosted. Artifact Contests used to have their own section due to being hosted on different forums/groups, but as of May 20, 2018 are now remerged back in due to the Artifact Contest designation no longer being applied to new contests.

Backups of important contest information can be found on the Contest Local Backup page. Backups of judging (spreadsheets, Pastebin, etc) can be found at the Judging Archives.
