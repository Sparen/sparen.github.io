GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

My RaNGE 17 Contest Entry is a standard Touhou-esque vertical scrolling shooter. Like my RaNGE 16 Contest Entry, the system is directly based off of that of SeitenTouji AMB and the script therefore comes loaded with extra features. There are two difficulties - Normal and Hard, and two players - my Artifact 2 Marisa and Ultima's DDC Reimu.

Default starting lives are configurable, and extends are gained every three life fragments (life fragments are obtained when not dying to an attack, spell fragments if you die to an attack).
