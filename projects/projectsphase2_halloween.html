GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

My Halloween Contest Entry is a standard Touhou-esque vertical scrolling shooter. There are five difficulty options - Lunatic is nerfed and a Phantasm mode features a failed attempt to make the script 'spookier'.

As a standard boss battle, there are no real gimmicks, although some individual spells feature various... 'gimmicks' (if they can be called that). The recommended player is my 0.12m Kogasa Player script, although the script is balanced for standard players. There are three nonspells and three spells.

POSTMORTEM

[November 23, 2015]

This was a radical departure from my Sanae boss battle in that originality was almost completely replaced by a burning desire to be a recreation of the original with an original twist. I think I did a better job of recreating the spell and 3D backgrounds and danmaku than anything else though. In the end, it wasn't particularly scary and the gimmicks were not particularly enjoyable. However, this is by no means a bad script.
