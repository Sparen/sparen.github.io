RANGE 11 (AAA): NUCLEAR HELL RAVEN



RELEASE INFORMATION

Release Date: May 4, 2014

Download: Download RC-1 (May 4, 2014)

Install: Place the unarchived directory within the 'script' directory of your copy of Touhou Danmakufu ph3 [.1]

Official Post: Click Here for Information

Contest Page: Click Here for Information



GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

My AAA Contest Entry is a standard Touhou-esque vertical scrolling shooter with two difficulty options. There are no gimmicks to note. The background is sort of hideous and there is no way to toggle effects for this script because without the fog, you can't see any bullets in the non spells because my stage background is like... white.

A part of the script features Satori-inspired attacks (AKA recollections). There is a Satori-inspired nonspell and three recollections - Suiroga, Kanako, and Nitori.

ADDITIONAL NOTES

A sharp contrast from my other ph3 scripts, this script ranks more like my Sekibanki battle - it has an amazing background and amazing music but the danmaku is about as unimaginative and uninspiringly unoriginal as it gets. I don't understand how something this mediocre managed to co-win third place in a contest, but whatever. It was also pretty hard to replicate Satori's nonspell (I failed). In the end, my Suiroga and Virtue of Wind God cameos were what dragged this script down. Well, at least I know not to try those again... Too much ADD rendering. (The positive thing is that the sun code here has stayed with me ever since. If you like tasks with 20 parameters, that code is for you.)
