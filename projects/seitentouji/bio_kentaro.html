General Information

Personality

Kentaro is a hyperactive and curious boy who likes finding out about the abilities of others. He also loves games, anime, manga, and werewolves. Surprisingly kinky too.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Kentaro was an ordinary high school student who loved to draw and immerse himself in various forms of media. However, his parents disowned him when the police came to their house over his pirating of multiple anime series and he spent a year in juvenile detention before committing suicide. Somehow, his body was obliterated during the process.

Character Design

Origin

Kentaro was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His chosen last name, Kibaarashi, literally means fang storm, while his slightly altered name, Kentaro, replaces whatever kanji used to be there (Ken) with the kanji for dog (also Ken).

Design

Kentaro is of average height for an ordinary high schooler, with silvery gray hair, ears, and tail, similarly to a wolf in coloration. His tail is rather bushy and wags when he's excited.

Kentaro is rather muscular, and he wears a tank top and shorts. His tank top is plain but he also has AWOO, Momiji, and other assorted designs in his closet. His shorts are rather ordinary, except that there's a hole for his tail.
