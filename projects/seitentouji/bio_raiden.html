General Information

Personality

Raiden is the overly cautious type, preparing things in advance and always ready to deal with problems. He does his job, and he does it well.

Abilities

Raiden is able to create barriers.

Raiden's attack style is to be determined

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he is still guarding the factory as Taiga's subordinate.

Backstory

Raiden was originally a soldier in the military, who died in a particularly gruesome fashion. Upon reincarnation in Daiten Daichi, he was given a corporeal form at the Factory. Since then, he has formed a strange relationship with Taiga.

Character Design

Origin

Raiden was originally a generic robot midboss until given a name and ability. Design details and plot relevance were fleshed out on May 29, 2015. More details were added over the course of development, as was the decision to have him be more significant to the backstory of the world.

Name

His chosen name, Raiden, refers to electricity.

Design

Raiden uses a robotic android body, which has been altered to better reflect his actual body before death. He wears the standard uniform of the Factory. He has short orangish hair and brown eyes.

Relationships

Taiga Yoryou

Raiden has an interesting relationship with Taiga in that he is technically Taiga's subordinate but ends up doing Taiga's job because Taiga is often too lazy to do it himself. However, he doesn't mind, and the two share a relationship best explained as a very unromantic shonen-ai.
