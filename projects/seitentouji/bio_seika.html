Seika Koudan

Seika Koudan (鋼弾　生花 Koudan Seika) is the Extra Stage Boss of SeitenTouji 2 - Stolen Secrets of Hope. Ryusuke's cousin, he's a strange person with two separate personalities.

General Information

Personality

Seika has two personalities in the same body - one that's very masculine (Seika M) and one that's very feminine (Seika F). Seika M is outgoing, assertive, and enjoys physical activities. Seika F is very caring and eloquent, yet is also overly dramatic at times.

The two personalities, surprisingly, get along well, and Seika tends to get along well with all kinds of people, though Seika F's tendency to dress as a girl is off-putting to many of Seika M's friends. Surprisingly, perhaps, Seika M is more romantic than Seika F.

Seika is an excellent cook and cares deeply for his little cousin.

Abilities

Seika trained using a staff as a weapon back when he was alive. He carries around a three-part Bo-staff, and each personality uses it in a different way, with Seika M using it in its split form and Seika F using it in its whole form. In Daiten Daichi, Seika gained the ability to create strange force fields capable of repelling matter, and applies these fields to his staff.

Seika's attack style is yet to be determined but it will likely involve force field barriers that will move the player, bullet repulsion, and a heavy emphasis on dualism and curvy lasers.

Backstory

Seika was on his way back from a martial arts class when he was shot in a gay bar.

Character Design

Origin

Seika was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

Seika's last name is the same as Ryusuke's. Seika's legal first name is 成果, while in Daiten Daichi that is the name Seika M uses. In Daiten Daichi, Seika F uses the name 生花.

Design

Seika is a somewhat muscular yet thin young man with twin tails made from his brownish blonde hair. Seika M removes the hairbands and lets the hair fall naturally.

Seika's Appearances

SeitenTouji 2 - Stolen Secrets of Hope

Seika is the Extra Stage Boss of Stolen Secrets of Hope. For the first half of the boss battle, Seika F battles, but after a dialogue event, he transitions to Seika M, undoing his hair and removing his dress, choosing to fight in his boxers.

Relationships

Ryusuke Koudan

As Ryusuke's cousin, Seika takes good care of Ryusuke. Ryusuke, however, is terrified of Seika F. Their relationship will be further explained whenever I get to writing it.
