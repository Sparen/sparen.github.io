General Information

Personality

Rune is a chill and generally laid back kind of guy that likes to design and create stuff, whether it be sculpture, buildings, patterns and designs, or entire games.

Rune is typically quiet and can be narcissistic, especially due to the... fluffy extras integrated into his new body.

Abilities

Rune is able to create ice from water in the air and can freeze bodies of water solid. He is also an exceptional artist and designer specializing in finely executed detail.

Rune's attack style is TBD.

Occupation

As of the ending of Stolen Secrets of Hope, he is still living in the Den of the Brotherhood with Reito, helping design various new buildings and the like.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Rune was an Internet-based freelance artist and designer with a BS in Structural Engineering. He met his untimely fate when he was designing a sculpture for a park - some protesters against the park fired gasoline at the work area and then lit it on fire. He burned to death.

Upon reincarnation, he was given a mechanical body by Taiga and moved to the Village of Androids. However, the lifestyle didn't suit him and he soon left for Cherry Village. There he was the second test subject for Yutaro and Enrai's new research and was granted human form close to his physical body when he died. As an added bonus, they gave him fluffy fox ears and a fluffy fox tail, which he enjoyed due their pristine white fur.

Character Design

Origin

Rune was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His last name, Hyoga, literally means glacier, while his given name, Rune, literally means flowing cloud. Unlike many of Daiten Saitama's inhabitants, Rune still uses his actual name, which was invented by some... imaginative parents.

Design

Rune is a rather thin and tall young man with dyed light blue hair and blue eyes. His hair was originally blond, reflecting his European heritage. He takes care of his new body and therefore, he's lean and surprisingly strong physically.

Rune wears a hoodie with the name of his new studio's logo emblazoned on it over a T-shirt. He wears long and rather tight dark blue pants.

Rune has soft fluffy white arctic fox ears and a poofy arctic fox tail, which he takes good care of.
