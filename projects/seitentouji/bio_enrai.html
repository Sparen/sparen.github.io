General Information

Personality

Enrai is an outgoing and friendly yet undeniably reckless Physics Ph.D armed with a railgun. He's approachable, generally nice, and super chill... except when you manage to piss him off.

Abilities

Enrai is capable of manipulating magnetic fields and uses so to change the direction of his railgun projectiles.

Enrai's attack style is TBD.

Occupation

As of the ending of Stolen Secrets of Hope, he is still working in the Cherry Village alongside Yutaro.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Enrai was a new Ph.D who worked closely with engineers. He helped build numerous tools and the like, but found life as a Post-Doc stressful and eventually got hit by a car when he was crossing the street half-asleep.

Character Design

Origin

Enrai was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His last name, Eisetsu, literally means snow shadow, while his given name, Enrai, literally means eternal lightning.

Design

Enrai is tall and lanky, with messy spiked dark blue hair, blue eyes, and PPE-level orange goggles that would probably look better on a Digimon protagonist half Enrai's age.

Enrai wears a sleeveless blue vest over a red T-shirt with a physics meme on it. He wears long dark-green cargo pants with lots of pockets and has flashy red sneakers. He's right handed and has built his personal railgun with that in mind.
