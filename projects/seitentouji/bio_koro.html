Koro Sanjou

Koro Sanjou (三条　転 Sanjou Koro) is the Stage 1 Midboss and Boss of Adamantine Memory Blade. He stays by the scrap fields, honing his abilities alone.

General Information

Personality

Koro is a calm, relatively uninteresting person with a neutral and ambivalent mindset. A loner, he can sort of be seen as a hermit of sorts, as he lives far from society and lives alone.

Abilities

He exhibits the ability to teleport small objects, noted by purplish spiral voids. He is skilled at drawing energy into his hands to form danmaku.

Koro's attacks depend on moving danmaku using voids.

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he is still living at the scrapyards.

Backstory

Koro was an ordinary boy who lived with an ordinary family. However, one day he was camping and never returned to the campsite...

After reincarnation, he basically stayed put in the scrapyards, watching the skies.

Character Design

Origin

Koro was created along with the other non-robot cast members for stages 1-5.

Name

His name, Koro, refers to translocation and his ability to move objects. When he was still alive, he may have gone by a different kanji, but in Daiten Daichi, he assumed his current identity.

Design

Koro still wears the same clothes he wore the day he arrived in Daiten Daichi, though with significant wear and tear. He has brown hair and eyes.

Koro is of average height for a high school teenage male and is skinny.
