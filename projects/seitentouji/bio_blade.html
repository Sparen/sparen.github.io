Blade Kousoku

Blade Kousoku (高速　ブレード Kousoku Bureedo) is the Stage 1 Boss of Stolen Secrets of Hope.

General Information

Personality

Blade is a diligent and strict person who enforces rules harder than most ordinary law enforcement. Still, he's got a nice side too.

Abilities

Blade is able to slice through air and is able to fly at supersonic speeds. He's also highly intelligent.

Blade's attack style is TBD.

Occupation

As of the ending of Stolen Secrets of Hope, he is still overseeing the now-operational PRT railway.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Blade was an engineer in charge of emerging technologies. However, he died when a crane in a cargo loading facility broke down, and he was splattered by a cargo container filled with stuffed animals.

Character Design

Origin

Blade was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His last name, Kousoku, is a reference to his speed, while his name, Blade, refers to his ability to slice through air.

Design

Blade's android body is modified similarly to Maryuu, and he boasts artificial skin, artificial hair, and the like. He's tall and thin, with spiky silvery hair and blue eyes.
