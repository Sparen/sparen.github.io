General Information

Personality

Yutaro is a respectable and polite man with a calm demeanor. He is diligent and always working his ass off.

Abilities

Yutaro has the ability to project a spirit's soul, thereby allowing him to display the contents of given soul. This allows him to determine the physical 'body' associated with a soul. Using his other ability - that of biochemical control - he is capable of thereby forming that body or integrating it into that of another existing compatible container. As his knowledge is nowhere near sufficient enough to generate a human body normally, it is thanks to his abilities that he is able to form bodies for souls or integrate biological components into mechanical bodies.

Yutaro's attack style comprises stylized DNA-based techniques. Helixes and chains comprise many of his attacks.

Occupation

As of the ending of Stolen Secrets of Hope, he is still living in the Cherry Village, working together with Akitora and Taiga to provide androids with living bodies.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Yutaro was a MD-PhD who was in his final year of residency when an arsonist set the hospital on fire. He helped with the evacuation but breathed in too much smoke and suffocated to death.

Character Design

Origin

Yutaro was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His last name, Nemuri, literally means sleepless, while the first kanji in his given name means reason.

Design

Yutaro is a tall and lanky man, like his assistant Enrai. He wears a lab coat over a long-sleeved shirt and wears long khakis.

Yutaro has soft brown hair and brown eyes, and has perpetual eye bags below them.
