General Information

Personality

Reito is a hardcore gamer who, despite his laid back habits, happens to be really really good at telling people what to do. He's a 100%er and very meticulous about things being done efficiently and on-time. That said, he's super nice and friendly when he's not playing something.

Abilities

Reito is able to make spirit projections of whatever his mind thinks of, and can use these as danmaku to attack. Other than that, Reito is exceptionally good at management and logistics, and never forgets the small details.

Reito's attack style is TBD.

Occupation

As of the ending of Stolen Secrets of Hope, he is still living in the Den of the Brotherhood with Rune, helping organize the members of the Brotherhood.

Backstory

Before reincarnation, Reitou was a Let's Player on YouTube who 100%ed games, especially smaller indie games. On the reality side, he was a college student managing several clubs and organizations at once, and was a Computer Science major focusing on Systems Engineering.

Reito died of a heart attack in his sleep after working for 40 straight hours during Finals Week - mainly due to his attempts to understand the Linux kernel - something that is not recommended during Finals Week.

Once in Daiten Daichi, he used his studies to create a high level Operating System - ReitOS, which became the foundation for many higher level advancements as it replaced the extremely low level systems in place in many cases and provided a much needed foundation upon which software could be effectively written. It was made to be compatible with the standard low level programming language of Daiten Daichi - D*.

Character Design

Origin

Reito was created on January 29, 2017 along with all other SSH characters.

Name

His last name, Aoba, is just a generic name, while his given name, Reito, literally means spirit blade.

Design

Reito is of average height and despite his thin appearance under a hoodie and shorts, he works out and, similarly to Rune, is physically capable and has some amount of muscle mass. He has smooth and rather unkempt silvery hair and blue eyes.
