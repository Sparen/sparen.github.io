Dyne Kairei

Dyne Kairei (海嶺　打陰 Kairei Dain) is the Stage 6 Boss of SeitenTouji 1 - Adamantine Memory Blade, as well as a future playable character. A new arrival to Daiten Daichi, he found a power he was unable to control.

General Information

Personality

Dyne's personality is yet to be determined

Abilities

Dyne is an excellent close-combat fighter, though his abilities in Daiten Daichi also involve the usage of shadows to create semimaterial energy-based weaponry that deals physical damage.

His fighting style is to be determined

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he is repenting for what he has done, traveling around Daiten Daichi separately from Teiki and Ryusuke.

Backstory

Dyne was a martial artist in training back when he was alive. Skilled and powerful, he rose through the ranks and by the time he was leaving high school, was well-respected. However, he had a concussion when an angry parent punched him down a staircase. He was reincarnated into Daiten Daichi, where he found a strange but elegant sword...

Character Design

Origin

As the last character in Adamantine Memory Blade to be given a name (name given and bio written on June 21, 2015), Dyne has the recognition of being the most procrastinated-on character in the game. His original martial artist background was determined about a month earlier, as were initial design elements.

Name

Literally meaning "Striking Shade Ridge", Dyne's name was made by selecting a letter that hadn't been used as the first letter of someone's name (D), and then using Google Translate to generate a name based on the initial personality and design elements for the character.
