General Information

Personality

Taiga is a cheerful and friendly person who tends to slack off, though he can get very serious.

Abilities

Taiga is able to absorb and discharge electricity from his body. Basically, he is able to use his body as a capacitor.

Taiga's attack style is to be determined, though it will probably be gimmicked and involve his attacks gaining power depending on how much damage is inflicted on him or something like that, since Teiki's ability is to create electricity.

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he is still overseeing the factory.
