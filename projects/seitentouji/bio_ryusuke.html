Ryusuke Koudan

Ryusuke Koudan (鋼弾　龍助 Koudan Ryuusuke) is a deuteragonist of the SeitenTouji Project, along with Teiki Tetsurou. Having arrived in Daiten Daichi relatively recently, he is unfamiliar with the world he has arrived in.

General Information

Personality

Ryusuke is a somewhat lonely and very loyal person who, although he tries to be fierce and powerful, would rather cuddle with kittens than fight. He's quite cheerful, yet melancholy.

Abilities

Given his current form, it would seem that he has a number of superhuman abilities, and this is in fact the case, but not to a great extent. While his mechanical limbs have very high durability, the advantages do not extend much farther than that.

Ryusuke's attacks are based on manipulating flame - he carries a flamethrower around with him and then manipulates the flames with a currently undisclosed method.

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he lives with Teiki and Gen in their house.

Backstory

Ryusuke was a normal boy until when one day, he was riding on a light rail vehicle when a car being driven by a drunk driver smashed into the vehicle he was in, resulting in his legs being amputated by a sharp piece of metal. After that, he was wheelchair ridden and for the next 10 years of his life, he grew farther from his friends due to his physical condition. On the 10th anniversary of the accident, he was hit by a car a block away from the old crash site, and he died, being reincarnated into Daiten Daichi.

Character Design

Origin

Ryusuke was created along with the other non-robot cast members for stages 1-5, although the concept for his character was imagined beforehand. As Stage 5 boss, he was chosen to be a future player character.

Name

Literally meaning "Steel Bullet Dragon Helper", Ryusuke's name, just like Teiki's, was thrown together in a vain attempt to come up with something decent.

Design

Ryusuke is relatively tall, although he is slightly shorter than Teiki. He has whitish and black hair, and has blue eyes. He has burn marks all around his abdomen from his unfortunate and various accidents with moving vehicles, which extend downwards to where his mechanical legs connect with the rest of his body. He covers most of these with dark flaming pants, which make the marks seem less like burn scars and more like tattoos or paint/style.

Ryusuke is rather thin, though the human portion of his body is toned and rather muscular due to his training.

Ryusuke's Appearances

SeitenTouji 1 - Adamantine Memory Blade

Ryusuke is the Stage 5 Boss and Stage 6 Midboss, and is loyal to the final boss. He tries to block Teiki from investigating further but fails miserably. After the events of the game, he decides to stick with Teiki.

Relationships

Teiki Tetsurou

Ryusuke has a great attachment to Teiki, mainly because of his many years of isolation from others. Teiki acts as Ryusuke's cuddle buddy whenever kittens are not around.

Gen'eimaru Hibyou

Ryusuke has a strange relationship with Gen, as Ryusuke loves cats. Gen is, for all practical purposes, Ryusuke's pet. See Gen's page for their special attack, 'Phantom Strike'.
