Taro Oshisou

Taro Oshisou (多思想　太郎 Ooshisou Tarou), original name Taro Komaeda (小前田　太郎 Komaeda Tarou) is the Stage 2 Midboss and Boss of Adamantine Memory Blade.

General Information

Personality

Taro is an enigmatic person who seems overly excited about everything yet delves into the darkest of thought experiments. He seeks power of the occult and arcane sorts, yet seems to simply pick up pieces of machinery with complete disregard to anything else.

Abilities

Taro has no special abilities.

Taro's attack style involves turning various though experiments into a semitangible form. He tends to either attack directly or sit back and have 'familiars' do the work for him.

Occupation

As of the ending of Adamantine Memory Blade, he is still wandering around.

Backstory

Born Taro Komaeda, he was an ordinary kid in an ordinary family. It is unknown how he died, but it seems that he was responsible for the pillaging of a number of classmates...

After reincarnation, he stayed away from society, drawn to power.

Character Design

Origin

Taro was created along with the other non-robot cast members for stages 1-5. Later on, when his initial spell and attack concepts were developed, it was decided that he would have a few screws loose somewhere. Much later, on May 24, 2015, his name was changed to match this and to avoid insulting real life people with the same name - he was initially planned to be an overly generic, relatable type of person.

Name

His self-given last name, Oshisou, refers to thought and his ability to express thought experiments as danmaku.

Design

Taro has short black hair with a blue tinge, and boasts a leanish build. In AMB, he appears with a shirt depicting the Trolley Problem as well as pants depicting a PVC (permanent virtual circuit) and shoes whose color scheme usually tends to invoke a memory of a bad shopping experience. Overall, he stands out and looks really horrible, partly due to Sparen's terrible color combination abilities.
