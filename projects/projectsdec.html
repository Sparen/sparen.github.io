Digital Earth: Conquest



Release Date: May 2, 2015

Official Post: Click Here for Information (and Download)



Digital Earth: Conquest was my first major Danmakufu ph3 project. Freed of the restrictions of 0.12m, I was able to go full force on literally everything, resulting in a game full of gorgeous (though simple) 3D backgrounds, plenty of amazing spellcards, and more.

You play as either Flamon or Strabimon, entering a tourney and battling against some of the denizens of the Digital World. As they travel through forests, mountains, and more, they uncover the truth behind the tourney, and meet new friends. The main gimmick involves collecting DigiEnergy via grazing or by special items, which allows Flamon and Strabimon to Digivolve up to two stages, adding powerful attacks to their existing ones.

DEC is a game of beautiful (although sometimes very hard) danmaku, challenge, and hopefully, fun. All music is from Digimon games, as I was unable to get original music, but in the end, it gives the characters more depth and personality than my poor attempt at dialogue ever could.

There were many times I when wanted to give up on this game, but I am glad I didn't, and it's amazing to see how far I have come.
