RANGE 12 (ICM)
THE AVATAR OF MOUNTAINS AND LAKES



RELEASE INFORMATION

Release Date: November 2, 2014

Download: Download RC-2 (November 2, 2014)

Install: Place the unarchived directory within the 'script' directory of your copy of Touhou Danmakufu ph3 [.1]

Official Post: Click Here for Information

Contest Page: Click Here for Information

GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

My ICM Contest Entry is a standard Touhou-esque vertical scrolling shooter with two difficulty options. There are no gimmicks to note unless the 3D danmaku that I copy-pasted from my LOCAA 6 counts as a gimmick (albeit an ineffective one).

ADDITIONAL NOTES

This project was my attempt at regaining my former greatness, although in the end, it turned out to simply be one of my 'better' scripts. Although most of the script was unimaginative (in my opinion) since I was either trying to emulate Talos's style (which I executed reasonably poorly) or reusing ideas, the script managed to be pretty good.
