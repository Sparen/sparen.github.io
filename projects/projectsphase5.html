Snom Player

Release Date: April 5, 2020

Download Link: Link

The player for Snomnomnom ~ The Trial of Snomsnom. A slow player that excels in bullet cancelling. Familiars destroy blue and green bullets depending on their color (outer ones destroy blue and inner ones destroy green). During the bomb, red deletable bullets are turned against the enemy and deal damage. This player does not work well with off-the-shelf scripts due to its gimmick and very slow speed and if other scripts by third parties want to use this player, care must be taken to design patterns to account for this (as well as not use bullet IDs that do not correspond to shots in the shotsheet).

Eleburstyr Player

Release Date: Sept 07, 2019

Download Link: Link

Eleburstyr is the player script for my Bullet Hell Artistry contest entry. It's an elemental familiar with the following attributes:

1. Rotating Shottype
Shottype rotates between four elements based on time. Fire is a standard forward focus shot, Water is a wide spread shot dealing very high point blank damage, Wind/Air is a pseudo-homing shot where shots underneath or above an enemy rocket towards them and do extra damage, and Earth is a forward burst shot type where occasional burst shots deal much of the damage

2. Basic Bomb
Eleburstyr has 4 bombs by default. These only clear bullets in a wide radius, and do not do any damage

Notes:
- Eleburstyr is a player that is HIGHLY dependent on enemy position. The player has NOT been tested on stage scripts, and does extremely poorly on bosses that like to move around juuuust a little but very frequently
- Eleburstyr has been equipped with backwards shots due to some gimmicks I added near the end of my contest entry
