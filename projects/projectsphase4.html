ph3 Projects

Below are some of my ph3 projects, listed in order of start/completion date (most recent at the top). These projects, along with the later phases of Digital Earth: Conquest and the SeitenTouji Project, are collectively known as Phase 4.

Danmakufu Wars: Sparen vs PhantomSong

Release Date: August 16, 2015

Official Post: Click Here for Information and Download



After a very long time, I finally decided to make another script that wasn't a full game. Done because I realized I wasn't having fun making scripts anymore, PhantomSong challenged me to a contest of sorts. You can find the full details in the Official Post, of course, but making it really helped me in many ways - I just feel like the danmaku was actually somewhat meaningful and hopefully original.

Incident Causer Mayhem Contest: The Avatar of Mountains and Lakes

Release Date: November 2, 2014

Official Post: Click Here for Information and Download



This project was my attempt at regaining my former greatness, although in the end, it turned out to simply be one of my 'better' scripts. Although most of the script was unimaginative (in my opinion) since I was either trying to emulate Talos's style (which I executed reasonably poorly) or reusing ideas, the script managed to be pretty good.
