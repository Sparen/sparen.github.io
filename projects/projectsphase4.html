ph3 Projects

Below are some of my ph3 projects, listed in order of start/completion date. These projects, along with the later phases of Digital Earth: Conquest, are collectively known as Phase 4.

Incident Causer Mayhem Contest: The Avatar of Mountains and Lakes

Release Date: November 2, 2014

Official Post: Click Here for Information and Download



This project was my attempt at regaining my former greatness, although in the end, it turned out to simply be one of my 'better' scripts. Although most of the script was unimaginative (in my opinion) since I was either trying to emulate Talos's style (which I executed reasonably poorly) or reusing ideas, the script managed to be pretty good.

LOCAA Contest 6: Danmakufu Troll Stars

Release Date: September 9, 2014

Official Post: Click Here for Information and Download



Arguably my best script in terms of sheer originality and creativity since RaNGE 10 Sanae. This is technically a troll script, but I assure you that although you are probably going to be trolled very hard, it is quite interesting and worth playing/watching other people fail at. In the description of the embedded video, there are links to other youtube videos of people doing runs of the script. This script took a few months to make but was definitely worth making. Have fun!
