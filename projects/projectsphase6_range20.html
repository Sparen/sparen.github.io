GAMEPLAY AND SYNOPSIS

My RaNGE 20 Contest Entry is a standard Touhou-esque vertical scrolling shooter. Like my RaNGE 16, 17, and 19 Contest Entries, the system is directly based off of that of SeitenTouji AMB and the script therefore comes loaded with extra features. There are two difficulties - Normal and Abnormal, and one player - my III Reisen.

Default starting lives default to a full set of 9 extra lives, and extends are gained every three life fragments (life fragments are obtained when not dying to an attack).
