Advertisement Policy

This page aims to clarify my advertisement policy regarding the advertisements located at the bottom of certain pages on this website.

The only legitimate advertisements are located solely on my ph3 tutorials pages. Advertisements on other pages are solely meant for humor purposes. There is no money being exchanged on any of the advertisements on this website.

Scripters may request that I feature their projects, blogs, and miscellaneous media in the Advertisements section of ph3 tutorials pages. My requirements are that the material is related to Touhou and/or Danmakufu, and that the material is safe for work.

To apply, please note that I will host up to five advertisements per page. You may request which page your material will be featured on. You must provide an image with dimensions of 4:1, and it must be hosted by an image hosting site such as Photobucket or ImageShack. I will show any given material only once on the site, and it may be removed without prior notice if the image link breaks or if the material the image links to breaks. The image should, if pointing to a source such as Youtube, Facebook, etc, have some indication of the destination.

As stated, this service is free. However, there are minimum quality standards that I will impose, and any material that is offensive will be removed. You may NOT host a link to nazr.in, tinyurl.com, adf.ly, or any other means of removing the target url from view. Please note that some pages have restrictions as to what types of advertisements I will allow.
