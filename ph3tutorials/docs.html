Introduction

Welcome to my local copy of the Touhou Danmakufu ph3 Documentation, available in case the Danmakufu Wiki goes down *again*. Here you can access the entirety of the translated Danmakufu function reference, although it has passed through the Sparen filter and therefore may be slightly different from the Wiki. Note that not all functions may be documented here, as I prioritize those used in the tutorials and the entire project is a work in progress expected to take a few months time to complete.

As a reminder, the OFFICIAL JAPANESE DOCUMENTATION can be found at mkm's website.
