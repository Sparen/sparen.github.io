Welcome to AFCDTech, Andrew Fan/Sparen's Website!

Welcome to my Github Pages site! This site was made in order to showcase and advertise projects that I, Andrew Fan (known as Sparen elsewhere on the internet) have made, as well as promote knowledge and usage of the scripting engine and associated language of Touhou Danmakufu ph3. Here you will find my projects, my Youtube and Advertisement services for the Danmakufu community, and my tutorials for Touhou Danmakufu ph3. On the left of each page, there is a navigation bar, and there are links and advertisement space at the bottom of almost every page.

Please feel free to browse around - maybe you'll learn something about Danmakufu or find an interesting video embedded somewhere.

My official Project Release Thread on Maidens of the Kaleidoscope can be accessed using the following link: Sparen's Code Dump.

You will find all of my Touhou and Danmakufu projects in the opening post of the thread linked above, although you can also visit my Projects page for embedded videos and brief information. I do not release projects on this website - all of my projects are announced on the linked MotK thread and all of my publicly released projects are on Bulletforge.

For more information about me, please visit my About Me page. Note that it is not a resume or CV.

What to advertise your Touhou website/blog/etc. on this website free of charge? Visit my Advertisements page for more details!

Want to read the infamous LOCAA Fanfiction? Read at your own risk here!

P.S. This site is NOT DESIGNED FOR MOBILE. I use preformatted code blocks and embedded youtube videos, which are not supported by browsers (code blocks completely mess up everything in mobile and iOS does not support embedded Youtube videos because of Flash).
